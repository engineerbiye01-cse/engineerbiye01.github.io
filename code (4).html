Hadiths Collection (Sample)

  • Sahih Bukhari: "The Prophet (ﷺ) said: 'None of you will have faith till he wishes for his (Muslim) brother what he likes for himself.'" (Arabic: لا يؤمن أحدكم حتى يحب لأخيه ما يحب لنفسه)
  • Sahih Muslim: "The Prophet (ﷺ) said: 'The strong is not the one who overcomes the people by his strength, but the strong is the one who controls himself while in anger.'" (Arabic: ليس الشديد بالصرعة، إنما الشديد الذي يملك نفسه عند الغضب)

Duas Collection (Sample)

  • Dua for Forgiveness: Astaghfirullah (أستغفر الله) - I seek forgiveness from Allah.
  • Dua for Protection: Allahumma innaka afuwwun tuhibbul afwa fa'fu anni (اللهم إنك عفو تحب العفو فاعف عني) - O Allah, You are Forgiving and love forgiveness, so forgive me.
